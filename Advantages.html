AI Importance

AI image is important because it can:

Drive insights in drug discovery

Help with decision making such as identifying a menu of likely treatment options for patients

Provide basic assistance, such as lane keeping while driving or text-to-speech based on images from a phone for the visually impaired

Help algorithms learn from object detection, recognition and segmentation

Automatically reduce blurred backgrounds, which results in clearer photos

Increase productivity by creating stunning visuals in seconds, without having to learn complex editing software

Give more control over your images by easily adjusting the textures, lighting, and shadows to get the exact look you want
